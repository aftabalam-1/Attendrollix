The Dashboard is the first screen you see after logging in. It gives a real-time snapshot of today's attendance across your entire college.

👨‍🎓
Total Students
Total number of registered students. Click to see the full list.
✅
Present Today
Students marked present today and the attendance percentage. Click to see names and times.
❌
Absent Today
Students who are absent. Click to see the full list.
⏰
Late Today
Students who arrived late and their entry times.
✨
Every stat card is clickable. Click any card to open a popup listing all students in that category — complete with roll number, course, and time.
Today's Activity & Course Overview

The right panel shows recent attendance activity. Use the Course dropdown at the top of the panel to filter the list to a single course.

The left panel shows a Course Overview with a student count and progress bar for each course. Click any course row to go directly to that course's student list.

🌙
Light / Dark mode: Click the 🌙 / ☀️ icon in the top-right corner to switch themes. This button is also available on the login screen.
Step 4 04 / 08

Managing Courses

Click Courses in the sidebar. Each course appears as a card. Hover over a card to reveal Edit and Delete buttons.

➕
Add a New Course
Click + Add Course in the top-right corner. Enter a Course Name (e.g. B.Tech), a Course ID (a short lowercase code, e.g. btech), and choose a colour. Click Add Course.
✏️
Edit a Course
Hover over a course card to reveal the Edit button. You can update the name and colour — the Course ID cannot be changed after creation.
🗑
Delete a Course
Hover and click Delete. Deleting a course only removes the course record. Students are not deleted — their data remains intact.

Step 6 06 / 08

Marking Attendance

Click Mark Attendance in the sidebar. Today's date is displayed automatically. Choose between Manual mode and QR Scan mode.

Method 1 Manual Attendance

Select Manual mode. Three buttons appear next to each student. Click the appropriate button to mark their status:

✓ Present ⏰ Late ✕ Absent

A coloured badge appears once a student is marked. Made a mistake? Click Reset to remove the record and mark again.

🔍
Course Filter: Use the course filter tabs below the mode selector to display only students from one course. Click All to show everyone.
Method 2 QR Code Attendance

Select QR Scan mode. A panel opens with three tabs:

⌨️
Enter QR ID
Type or paste the student's QR code and press Enter. The student is instantly marked Present.
📷
Scan Camera
Click "Start Camera". The browser will request camera permission. Point the camera at the student's QR code to auto-scan.
📤
Upload Image
Select a QR code image file from your device. The system will attempt to decode it automatically.
🛡️
Duplicate Protection: If a student's QR is scanned more than once on the same day, the system blocks the duplicate and shows a warning message.
Step 7 07 / 08

Attendance Reports

Click Reports in the sidebar. Three report ranges are available, all pulling live data from the database.

📅
Date Wise
Pick any date from the calendar to see every student's status and entry time for that day.
📊
6 Months
Attendance percentage for every student over the last 6 months, shown as a colour-coded progress bar.
📈
1 Year
Full-year trend. Students below 75% are automatically flagged with a warning.
Attendance Percentage Colour Coding
75% and above — Satisfactory / Excellent (green)
50–74% — Borderline (amber)
Below 50% — At Risk ⚠️ (red)

Most Indian universities (including Pune University) require a minimum of 75% attendance to be eligible for examinations. Attendrollix flags any student below this threshold automatically.

Help

Frequently Asked Questions

Common questions and their answers.

I forgot my password. What do I do?
+
The current version does not have an automated password reset flow. Contact your system administrator, or register a new account using a different email address. Make sure to keep your credentials stored safely in a password manager.
Does deleting a course delete the students in it?
+
No. Deleting a course only removes the course record. All student records and their attendance history are kept in the database and are unaffected.
I marked the wrong attendance status. How do I fix it?
+
On the Mark Attendance page, click the Reset button next to the student. This removes today's record. You can then mark the correct status. Resetting only affects today — historical records are not changed.
Where can I find a student's QR code?
+
Open the course the student is enrolled in, then click the 👁 View button next to their name. The popup shows the QR code image (requires an internet connection to load from qrserver.com) and the QR code text ID below it. Students can scan this from their identity card.
The camera scan is not working. What should I do?
+
First, make sure you have granted camera permission to the browser (click the lock icon in the address bar → Camera → Allow). If the camera still does not work, use the "Enter QR ID" tab instead — type or paste the student's QR code directly. You can also use the Upload Image tab to scan a saved QR image.
Does the app work on mobile?
+
Yes. Attendrollix is fully responsive. On mobile the sidebar opens via the ☰ hamburger menu. Camera QR scanning also works in mobile browsers — grant camera permission when prompted.
Is data saved permanently?
+
Yes. All data — colleges, courses, students, and attendance records — is stored permanently in a MySQL database on your local server (XAMPP or Laragon). Data persists across sessions and browser restarts as long as the database is running.
Pro Tips

Best Practices

Tips to make your daily workflow faster and more efficient.

⚡
Mark attendance right away
Mark attendance as soon as class begins. Use the Course Filter to focus on only one subject at a time.
📋
Check monthly reports
Review the 6-Month report at the end of each month. Give early warnings to students approaching the 75% threshold.
🔐
Store credentials safely
Save your email and password in a password manager. There is no self-service password reset in the current version.
📱
Use QR mode for large classes
For classes of 50 or more students, QR scanning is significantly faster. Make sure students have their QR code image available.
🎨
Assign distinct colours to courses
Give each course a unique colour during setup. This makes it easier to distinguish them at a glance on the Dashboard.
🔍
Use the stat card popups
Click the "Absent Today" card on the Dashboard each morning to see who is missing and follow up immediately.

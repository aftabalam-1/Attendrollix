Features

Everything Built for
Educational Institutions

A comprehensive system covering the full attendance lifecycle — from student onboarding to detailed analytics.

01📊
Live Dashboard
Real-time view of today's attendance — present, absent, and late counts with course-level breakdown and clickable detail modals.
02📱
QR Scanning
Each student gets a unique QR code. Scan or enter to mark present instantly — timestamped and duplicate-proof at database level.
03📈
Reports & Analytics
6-month and yearly attendance percentage per student with colour-coded risk indicators — red below 75%, green above 90%.

Multi-College
Full data isolation per institution. Each college manages only their own students and records — zero cross-contamination guaranteed.
05📚
Course Management
Add unlimited colour-coded courses. Default courses — B.Sc, B.Com, BCA, B.A, BBA — are created automatically on signup.
06🔐
Secure Auth
bcrypt-hashed passwords, PHP session authentication, login email alerts, and full password reset flow via secure email tokens.
Process

From Setup to Reports
in Four Steps

1
Register Your Institution
Create an account with your college name, email, and password. Five default courses are automatically created. Import database.sql first via phpMyAdmin.
~2 minutes setup
2
Add Your Students
Enter student name, roll number, and course. Each student receives a unique QR code (QR-S{id}-XXXXXXXX) generated automatically by the server.
Database-backed
3
Mark Daily Attendance
Use QR scan for instant marking or manual mode to set Present, Absent, or Late for each student. Time is auto-recorded. Duplicates blocked.
Real-time updates
4
Generate & Analyse Reports
Pull attendance percentage reports for any student over 6 months or 1 year. Spot patterns and identify at-risk students with visual progress bars.
Full history access

✅ Marking Attendance

Go to Mark Attendance in sidebar. Two modes:

  • Manual: Click Present, Absent, or Late for each student
  • QR Tab: Enter or scan QR code → student instantly marked present

📈 Reports

Go to Reports. Choose Date Wise, 6 Months, or 1 Year. See per-student attendance percentage — red below 75%, gold 50–74%, green above 75%.

🏫 College Profile

Update your college name, location, university board, and phone from College Profile in the sidebar.

🔑 Password Recovery

Login screen → Forgot Password → enter registered email → check inbox for reset link (valid 1 hour).
